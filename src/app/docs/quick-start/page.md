# **Introduction**

## **Quick Start**

Get up and running in minutes!

1. **Add Products** – Enter product name and price, or upload them in bulk.
1. **Start a Sale** – Search products by typing or using voice search, and add them to the cart.
1. **Assign a Customer** – Link an existing customer or add a new one.
1. **Accept Payment** – Choose payment method (Cash, Card, or Online).
1. **Print Receipt** – Instantly print or reprint receipts — even offline!
