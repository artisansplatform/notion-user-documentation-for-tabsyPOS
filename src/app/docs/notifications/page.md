# Notification Center

## Notifications



The **Notifications** tab displays system-generated alerts such as:

- Low stock alerts
- Out-of-stock products
- Inventory updates

These notifications are designed to **remind you to take action** rather than simply acknowledge them. When a product reaches a low stock level, the notification remains visible so that it is not overlooked.


To resolve a low stock notification, you can take actions such as:

- **Adding more stock** to the product, or
- **Disabling stock management** for that product if inventory tracking is no longer required.

Clicking on certain notifications may redirect you to the relevant page (for example, the filtered **Products** list) so you can quickly review and update the affected items.


Notifications ensure that important inventory issues remain visible until the necessary action is taken, helping you maintain accurate stock levels and avoid stockouts.
