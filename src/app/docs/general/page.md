# Frequently Asked Questions

## **General**


<details>
<summary>**What is TabsyPOS?**</summary>

TabsyPOS is a **modern, browser-based Point of Sale system** designed to simplify retail billing and store operations. It enables retailers to manage sales, products, customers, payments, and receipts seamlessly - both online and offline.


</details>

<details>
<summary>**Who is TabsyPOS designed for?**</summary>

TabsyPOS is ideal for **single-store retail businesses** looking for a simple, easy-to-use system to manage billing, products, and customers efficiently.


</details>
