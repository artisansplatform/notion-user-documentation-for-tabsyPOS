# Inventory Management

## Stop Managing Stock



If you no longer wish to track stock for a product:

1. Go to **Products**
2. Click on Manage Stock icon besides stock quantity
3. Click on “Stop Managing Stock”

![ef4dcf19-69e2-4ebb-8c86-cf8b1ce1d138.png](/images/docs/inventory-management/stop-managing-stock/2026-02-12T10:10:00.000Z.png)


The system will stop tracking stock movements for that product.
