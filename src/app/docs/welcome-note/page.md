# Welcome

## Welcome Note

Welcome to TabsyPOS, a simple and intelligent browser-based Point of Sale system built for single-store operations.
TabsyPOS lets you manage sales, products, and customers quickly and effortlessly — right from your browser. There’s no installation required, and it works even when you’re offline. All your data stays securely stored on your device, and you can easily back it up to Google Drive whenever needed.
With features like voice-based product search, AI-powered assistance, and smart data handling, TabsyPOS helps you focus on running your store — not your software.
This User Guide walks you through all the essential features, including how to create sales, manage products and customers, update settings, and back up your store data safely.
