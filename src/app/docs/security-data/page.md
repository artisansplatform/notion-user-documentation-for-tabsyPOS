# Frequently Asked Questions

## **Security & Data**


1. **Do we need local servers or complex hardware?**

    No. TabsyPOS does not require local servers or complex infrastructure, reducing operational overhead.

2. **How can I ensure my data is secure?**

    For enhanced data security, TabsyPOS allows you to **back up your exported files directly to Google Drive**. This ensures that your store information is **safely stored and easily restorable** whenever needed.

3. **How is my data backed up?**

    TabsyPOS provides **automatic cloud backup** using **Firebase** and also allows optional **Google Drive integration**. This ensures that all your sales, customer, and product data is **securely stored, recoverable, and protected against device loss or failure**.
