# Frequently Asked Questions

## **Security & Data**


<details>
<summary>**Do we need local servers or complex hardware?**</summary>

No. Tabsy POS does not require local servers or complex infrastructure, reducing operational overhead.


</details>

<details>
<summary>**How can I ensure my data is secure?**</summary>

For enhanced data security, Tabsy POS allows you to **back up your exported files directly to Google Drive**. This ensures that your store information is **safely stored and easily restorable** whenever needed.


</details>

<details>
<summary>**How is my data backed up?**</summary>

Tabsy POS provides **automatic cloud backup** using **Firebase** and also allows optional **Google Drive integration**. This ensures that all your sales, customer, and product data is **securely stored, recoverable, and protected against device loss or failure**.


</details>
