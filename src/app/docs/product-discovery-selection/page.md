# Sales Module

## Product Discovery & Selection



**Product Search Options**


TabsyPOS offers multiple ways to search for products quickly and efficiently.

- **Text Search:** You can type the product name or price directly into the search bar to instantly find matching items.
- **Voice Search:** You can use **Voice Search** through the AI chatbot icon located at the top-right corner of the screen. Simply click the microphone button inside the chatbot and speak the product name. TabsyPOS will recognise your voice input and display the matching products instantly.

    ![TabsyPOS-Smart-POS-System-11-28-2025_08_56_AM.png](/images/docs/sales-module/product-discovery-selection/2025-11-28T03:26:00.000Z.png)


**Instant Product Creation**

- If the searched product doesn’t exist in the system, you can **create it instantly** without leaving the Sales page.
- Simply click on  **“Add New Product”**  — enter the product name and price, and it will be automatically added to your Product List
